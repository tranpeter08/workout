"Tuna Salad Sandwich"

Ingredients:

  • 4 canned Tuna
  • 1 Tomato
  • 2 Slices Rye Bread
  • 1/4 cup Mayonnaise

Recipe:

  1. Open canned Tuna and drain water
  2. Mix tuna and mayonnaise
  3. Slice Tomato
  4. Toast Rye Bread
  5. Spread mayonaise on bread
  6. Stack ingredients and enjoy
